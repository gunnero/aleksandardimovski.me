# Confidentiality and portfolio policy

Public case studies may describe role, general context, responsibilities, technology categories, engineering approach, operational considerations, and carefully stated outcomes.

Never publish private source code, credentials, tokens, client contacts, customer or production data, private production URLs, infrastructure identifiers, confidential business rules, contractual details, or unsupported metrics and claims. Clearly label private work and sanitize Hera Backoffice and Nema30 Backoffice. A repository link may appear only after confirming the repository is public and approved for attribution.

Before release, review both source content and rendered output. When uncertain, omit the detail.
